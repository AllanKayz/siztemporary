Attention Young Surveyors!

Join the Survey Institute of Zimbabwe as a Student Member

Elevate Your Professional Journey

Hello, everyone! If you've recently graduated and are looking to elevate your professional journey, now is the perfect time to join the Survey Institute of Zimbabwe (SIZ) as a Graduate Member!

Why join SIZ?

  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with experienced professionals and peers in the surveying field.
  • Professional Development: Access to workshops, seminars, and resources that can enhance your skills.
  • Recognition: Being a member of a professional body adds credibility to your qualifications.
  • Career Advancement: Stay updated on industry trends and job opportunities.

Application Requirements

To apply, please fill in the form and attach the following:

  • Updated CV
  • Certified copies of certificates and transcripts
  • Certified copy of National ID

Kindly email your application to: surveyinstitutezimbabwe@gmail.com

Don't miss out on this opportunity to advance your career and be part of a dynamic community.
